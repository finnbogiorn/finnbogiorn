The Black Dahlia Murder - 2011 shirt

Logged March 7th, 2023

Black T-shirt printed on Jerzeez, 5-color screen print

This is a tour shirt from a 2011 run that the Michigan death metal icons did through the UK and a few select dates in Europe. What makes this shirt unique is the graphic which infringes copyright law, and so could not be reproduced following the run of shows.

I got this shirt from a friend from Ukraine after he found it at a thrift shop. He knew I had a strong fascination with The Black Dahlia Murder. Seeing them at Wacken 2012 and Warped Tour 2013 showed me how brilliant they were at transcending any scene and community. Rest in Peace Trevor Strnad, I miss you.
