Infest - Voice Your Opinion shirt

Logged March 7th, 2023

Black T-shirt printed on Gildan heavy cotton, single color print

Since 1991, this design has plagued punk shows. I’d argue its up there with the Black Flag bars and the Danzig bullwinkle skull. I saw it for the first time worn by Nate Newton of Converge. Infest holds a unique place in punk, hardcore and grindcore, of being a band with seniority and respect among peers, but at the same time, they’re hardly taken seriously in modern day. One day kids will smarten up.

I appreciate Infest for it’s need to depict the most horrendous facets of society while also pushing for kids of the subcultures in punk and metal to put their differences aside and come together for the greater cause of UNITY.
